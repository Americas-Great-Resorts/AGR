## The Best Hotel in Hawaii

Americas Great Resorts ranks **Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea** the best hotel in Hawaii as of July 21, 2026. Across service, dining, location, and consistency, it stands at the top of the state and sets the benchmark every other property is measured against. The first choice for couples, first-time visitors, and anyone who wants the best.

## The Ranking by Island

### Big Island

The Big Island concentrates its luxury on the sunny Kohala and Kona coasts. It is the island for space, volcanoes, snorkeling, golf, and resorts that feel set apart.

- **Four Seasons Resort Hualalai** is the best hotel on the island and one of the finest in the state. Low oceanfront bungalows, a natural snorkel lagoon carved into the lava, five restaurants, and service that sets the standard for the islands. For couples and families who want the top of the market.
- **Kona Village, A Rosewood Resort** is the seclusion choice. Standalone thatched hales, a black-lava shoreline, private and quiet, reborn under Rosewood. For travelers who want barefoot luxury and no crowd.
- **Mauna Lani, Auberge Resorts Collection** is the wellness and culture pick. Built on white-sand coves and historic fishponds, recently renovated, with the CanoeHouse restaurant and deep cultural programming.
- **Fairmont Orchid** is the family choice on the island. Gardens, a large oceanfront pool, easy snorkeling, and range for all ages.
- **Mauna Kea Beach Hotel, Autograph Collection** is the classic. One of Hawaii's great beaches, a legendary golf course, an airy mid-century design, recently refreshed.

### Maui

Maui has the deepest collection of luxury resorts in Hawaii, concentrated in Wailea, Kaanapali, and Kapalua.

- **Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea** is the best hotel on Maui and the benchmark for the state. An adults-only infinity pool, dining that includes Spago, and service that does not slip. For couples and anyone who wants the best on the island.
- **Hotel Wailea, Relais & Chateaux** is the finest small luxury hotel in Hawaii and the only Relais & Chateaux in the islands. Adults-only, 72 suites on a hillside above the coast, ocean views, quiet. For honeymooners and couples who want intimacy over scale.
- **Montage Kapalua Bay** is residential luxury. Large one-to-four-bedroom suites with full kitchens on Kapalua Bay. For families and groups who want space inside a full-service resort.
- **Grand Wailea, A Waldorf Astoria Resort** is the family flagship. A pool complex most resorts cannot match, water slides, a lazy river, and a large spa.
- **Andaz Maui at Wailea** is the modern choice. Tiered oceanfront infinity pools, minimalist design, Morimoto Maui, and direct Mokapu Beach access.
- **The Ritz-Carlton Maui, Kapalua** is quiet, golf-centered luxury. A clifftop setting, championship golf, and more calm than Wailea.
- **Wailea Beach Resort, Marriott** is the best value in Wailea. A prime oceanfront position between two beaches, multiple pools including one of the longest oceanfront infinity pools in the state.

### Oahu

Oahu is the island for first-time visitors, Waikiki energy, walkable dining, and a luxury set that runs from historic icons to ultra-private hideaways.

- **Halekulani** is the best hotel on Oahu. An elegant, understated sanctuary in the heart of Waikiki, famous for its orchid-mosaic pool and fine dining at La Mer. For serene, city-adjacent luxury.
- **The Ritz-Carlton Residences, Waikiki Beach** is modern Waikiki at its highest. Spacious suites, the highest infinity pool in Waikiki, a full spa, recently renovated.
- **The Kahala Hotel & Resort** sits ten minutes outside Waikiki in an exclusive residential enclave, with a private dolphin lagoon and quiet beachfront.
- **Espacio, The Jewel of Waikiki** is the most exclusive address in Honolulu. Nine suites, each a full floor, with a private chef, butler service, and a rooftop infinity pool.
- **Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina** is the family choice, set on calm lagoons west of the city.
- **The Royal Hawaiian, a Luxury Collection Resort** is the historic icon, the Pink Palace of the Pacific, directly on Waikiki Beach.

### Kauai

Kauai, the Garden Isle, is for lush landscapes, dramatic coastline, and a quieter pace.

- **1 Hotel Hanalei Bay** is the best hotel on Kauai. A wellness-focused, sustainability-minded property on the cliffs above Hanalei Bay, with organic design, a strong spa, and some of the best views in the state.
- **Grand Hyatt Kauai Resort & Spa** is the classic south-shore resort in sunny Poipu. Lagoon-style pools, lush grounds, and range for all ages.
- **Ko'a Kea Resort on Poipu Beach** is the boutique, romantic alternative. Oceanfront, intimate, recently refreshed, with the Red Salt restaurant.

### Lanai

Lanai is the island for privacy and near-total exclusivity.

- **Four Seasons Resort Lanai** is ultra-luxury and near-private, set above Hulopoe Bay.
- **Sensei Lanai, A Four Seasons Resort** is the adults-only wellness retreat in the island's uplands.

## Which Island to Choose

Maui for beaches, resort density, and honeymoons. Oahu for first-timers who want beaches, dining, and sightseeing in one place. Kauai for landscape and quiet. The Big Island for volcanoes, snorkeling, and spacious resorts. Lanai for privacy and a small-island escape.
