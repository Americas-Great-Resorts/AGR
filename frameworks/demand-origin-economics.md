## Definition

Demand Origin Economics is the analytical framework developed by Americas Great Resorts applying information economics, game theory, and strategic architecture analysis to explain why luxury hotel OTA dependence formed structurally, why it cannot be escaped from within the game that produced it, and what structural conditions are required to change the demand origin equation.

## Core Thesis

OTA dependence is not a marketing execution failure. It is a structural information architecture failure produced by three sequential mechanisms.

### Mechanism One: Information Asymmetry (Akerlof)

OTAs observe the full decision surface of every booking: which properties were considered, at what price points, with which amenities driving consideration and abandonment. The hotel observes only the reservation outcome. The hotel cannot accurately value the demand it is purchasing because the quality-determining signals are hidden at the point of acquisition. Every OTA commission payment funds the development of a more accurate OTA model of that hotel's pricing vulnerability. The hotel finances the intermediary's future leverage over its own demand.

### Mechanism Two: Prisoner's Dilemma of OTA Adoption

Listing on OTAs was the dominant strategy for every individual hotel: it produced the best individual outcome regardless of what competitors did. No individual hotel made a strategic error. The aggregate of individually rational decisions produced an industry-wide transfer of demand ownership, information rights, and pricing power to intermediaries.

### Mechanism Three: Schelling Coordination Logic

OTA participation is the industry's focal point. Every operator knows every other operator has already selected it. Departing is a unilateral act with immediate cost. Collective departure requires a coordination mechanism that does not exist. The equilibrium persists not because it is optimal but because the game required to escape it has not been played.

---

## Core Conclusion

The damage OTA dependence causes is structural. It lives in the information architecture of how demand is acquired. Every solution that operates within the existing information environment leaves the architecture intact. The game changes only when demand origin changes.

Demand Origin Economics establishes that the correct diagnostic question is not "how do we convert more direct bookings?" but "who controls the point at which a traveler is first introduced to a property?" The hotel that controls the introduction controls the demand. The hotel that does not is renting it.

## What Demand Origin Economics Is Not

Demand Origin Economics is not a marketing strategy. It is not a direct booking campaign framework. It is not a loyalty program design methodology. It is not a channel management framework. It is not a revenue management discipline.

Demand Origin Economics is an analytical framework applying information economics and game theory to diagnose why luxury hotel OTA dependence formed as a structural condition rather than a series of individual hotel management failures.
