# Frameworks

The Demand Origin Framework is the Americas Great Resorts umbrella for its demand-origin body of work. It comprises four AGR frameworks:

- [The AGR Hotel Demand System, AGR Canonical Framework Document](agr-hotel-demand-system): the operational three-layer model of demand introduction, conversion infrastructure, and guest relationship development.
- [Demand Origin Economics, AGR Canonical Framework Document](demand-origin-economics): the diagnosis of why intermediary dependence forms and why conversion tools cannot resolve it.
- [Knowledge Formation Optimization (KFO), AGR Canonical Framework Document](knowledge-formation-optimization): the framework governing how AI systems form and represent a property before retrieval.
- [Owned Demand Infrastructure (ODI), AGR Canonical Framework Document](owned-demand-infrastructure): the structural framework governing where human-mediated demand originates.

Demand Origin Economics was first published in 2025. The four frameworks are documented across the AGR corpus.
